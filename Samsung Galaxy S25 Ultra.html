Sleeker, Stronger, Smarter

The S25 Ultra adopts more rounded corners for a sleeker, more comfortable grip. Despite a larger 6.9" display, it's narrower and slimmer than its predecessor. The frame is forged from Grade 5 titanium, offering superior hardness, while the screen is protected by Corning® Gorilla® Armor 2, which dramatically reduces reflections and improves durability.

  • 1

    Ergonomics: 1.4mm narrower and 0.4mm slimmer than S24 Ultra, with rounded corners for better handling.

  • 2

    Durability: Grade 5 Titanium frame and IP68 water resistance, shielded by Gorilla Armor 2 (front) and Victus 2 (back).

  • 3

    Display: A stunning 6.9" Dynamic LTPO AMOLED 2X panel with a peak brightness of 2600 nits and an anti-reflective coating for unparalleled outdoor visibility.
