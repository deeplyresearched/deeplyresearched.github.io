About the Volvo EX30

A detailed look at the vehicle at the center of our analysis. The EX30 is Volvo's smallest, fastest, and most sustainable SUV to date, designed to appeal to a new generation of electric car buyers.

Scandinavian Design, Distilled

The EX30 embodies minimalist design principles. Its exterior features a confident face with a closed shield grille and a digital interpretation of the "Thor's Hammer" headlights. The long wheelbase and large wheels give it a balanced and powerful stance. Inside, the focus is on a simplified, calming experience, centered around a single 12.3-inch screen and a full-width soundbar, removing the need for traditional dashboard speakers.

Interior "Rooms"

Volvo offers four distinct, curated interior design themes called "rooms," which use a variety of renewable and recycled materials like flax, wool, and denim fibres.

Performance & Powertrain

  • Single Motor Extended Range

    Rear-wheel drive model with a 69 kWh battery, producing 268 hp. It accelerates from 0-60 mph in 5.1 seconds and targets an EPA-estimated range of around 275 miles.

  • Twin Motor Performance

    All-wheel drive model with the same 69 kWh battery, boasting a combined 422 hp. This version is Volvo's fastest-accelerating car ever, reaching 0-60 mph in just 3.4 seconds, with a targeted range of 265 miles.

Efficient Charging

The Extended Range variants can charge at speeds up to 153 kW on a DC fast charger, allowing them to replenish from 10% to 80% battery in approximately 26.5 minutes.

Technology & Safety

  • Centralized Infotainment

    A single 12.3-inch tablet screen runs on a next-generation system powered by Google's Android Automotive OS, featuring built-in Google Maps, Google Assistant, and wireless Apple CarPlay.

  • Safe Space Technology

    True to its brand, the EX30 is packed with safety features. This includes a special safety system for intersections and a door opening alert that warns if you're about to open the door into the path of a cyclist or scooter.

Driver Deep Dive

Explore the core attributes that define the Volvo EX30's market position. Each section below provides a detailed look at how the vehicle delivers on its promise of sustainability, price, technology, safety, and design.

Sustainability

The EX30 has the smallest carbon footprint of any Volvo to date. It utilizes a high percentage of recycled materials, including 17% recycled steel and aluminum, and 17% recycled plastics and textiles in the interior.

Price Competitiveness

With a starting price around $35,000, the EX30 enters the market as one of the most affordable premium electric SUVs. This pricing strategy is designed to attract new, younger buyers to the Volvo brand and accelerate EV adoption.

Advanced Tech

The vehicle features a single 12.3-inch tablet-style screen powered by Google's Android Automotive OS. This centralizes controls and infotainment, offering built-in Google Maps, Google Assistant, and access to the Play Store for a smartphone-like user experience.

Renowned Safety

Living up to the brand's reputation, the EX30 includes advanced safety systems like a new driver alert system that monitors face and eye movements, and a door-opening alert to prevent collisions with cyclists.

Distinctive Design

The EX30 embodies Scandinavian minimalism with a clean, modern aesthetic. It features a long wheelbase, large wheels, and equal overhangs. The interior uses innovative, sustainable materials to create four distinct design "rooms."

Market Context

The EX30 is crucial for Volvo's goal of becoming an all-electric carmaker by 2030 and achieving carbon neutrality by 2040. It targets a younger demographic, many of whom may be purchasing their first Volvo and first EV.
