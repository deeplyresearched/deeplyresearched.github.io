Hybrid Power, Supercar Soul

The Artura Spider is propelled by a 3.0L twin-turbocharged V6 engine paired with a potent Axial Flux electric motor. This combination delivers instant torque and a thrilling rush of power all the way to its 8,500 rpm redline. The lightweight MCLA carbon fibre chassis and revised adaptive dampers ensure this power is delivered with unerring accuracy and balance.

Sculpted by Air

The Artura Spider's design is a masterclass in functional elegance. Every curve, vent, and surface is meticulously crafted to manage airflow, enhance cooling, and create downforce, all while delivering a stunning aesthetic. Explore the key design elements that define this convertible supercar.

Aerodynamic Masterpiece

Hot-formed aluminum panels create a seamless, 'shrink-wrapped' body with minimal shutlines. Hidden door inlets and glazed rear buttresses channel air efficiently, while a rear spoiler and diffuser generate 50kg of downforce for high-speed stability. The signature dihedral doors complete the dramatic look.

For 2025, a new standard Stealth Badge Pack adds a subtle, aggressive touch with blacked-out front and rear badging.

Driver-Focused Cockpit

The interior is a minimalist, driver-centric environment. The instrument binnacle moves with the steering column, ensuring perfect visibility. Powertrain and Handling controls are mounted on the binnacle for fingertip access, keeping the steering wheel pure and free of buttons. Standard Clubsport seats provide excellent support, with more comfort-oriented options available.

Open-Top Freedom in 11 Seconds

The one-piece Retractable Hard Top (RHT) is an engineering marvel. Actuated by eight electric motors, it operates almost silently, opening or closing in just 11 seconds at speeds up to 31 mph. An optional electrochromic glass panel can block over 99% of sunlight at the touch of a button, offering the best of both worlds.

Intelligent Performance

The Artura Spider is packed with advanced technology to enhance performance, convenience, and safety. From its lightning-fast infotainment system to intelligent driver aids, explore the cutting-edge features that define the modern supercar experience.

Infotainment & Connectivity

+

The McLaren Infotainment System II (MIS II) runs on an 8-inch HD touchscreen, supporting Apple CarPlay. An optional 12-speaker Bowers & Wilkins audio system provides a premium sound experience, while a wireless smartphone charger adds convenience. Notably, Android Auto is not supported.

Driver Assistance

+

Standard features include Lane Departure Warning and Road Sign Recognition. The optional Driving Assistant Pack adds Blindspot Monitoring and Rear Cross-Traffic Detection, enhancing awareness. A standard vehicle lift system helps navigate speed bumps and steep driveways.

Hybrid & Chassis Tech

+

Four powertrain modes (E-Mode, Comfort, Sport, Track) tailor the car's character. An E-Differential optimizes power delivery, while Variable Drift Control (VDC) allows for controlled slides. The car also features Pirelli Cyber Tyre technology, providing real-time tire data for ultimate safety and performance.
