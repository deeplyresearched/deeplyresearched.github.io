A Synthesis of Form and Function

The YU7's design combines luxury aesthetics, aerodynamic efficiency, and thoughtful practicality.

Calculated Luxury

The exterior's resemblance to the Ferrari Purosangue is a strategic choice, instantly conveying high-performance luxury. This is supported by classic automotive proportions and a drag coefficient of just 0.245 Cd, achieved through features like active grille shutters and flush door handles.

  • ➔ 3.11 m² Clamshell Hood: The largest on any mass-produced vehicle, creating a seamless, premium look.
  • ➔ Evolved Halo Taillight: Uses ultra-red lighting for a dramatic visual signature and superior visibility.
  • ➔ Signature Colors: Offered in nine colors, including a distinctive Gemstone Green.

A Sanctuary of Comfort

The cabin is a blend of high-quality materials, advanced comfort features, and exceptional practicality. Nappa leather, double-paned glass for a quiet ride, and extensive storage solutions create a true luxury experience.

  • ➔ Zero Gravity Seats: Standard front seats with 10-point massage and up to 123° recline.
  • ➔ Expansive Storage: 141L frunk, up to 1,758L rear trunk, and 36 cabin compartments.
  • ➔ Smart Amenities: Optional 4.6L smart fridge and dual 80W wireless charging pads.

The Technology Core

Xiaomi's expertise in consumer electronics defines the YU7's intelligent experience.

"Human x Car x Home" Ecosystem

The YU7 is powered by Xiaomi's HyperOS, seamlessly integrating the vehicle with your digital life. It connects to Xiaomi smartphones, watches, and smart home devices, creating a unified, intelligent experience. The system also fully supports Apple CarPlay for broader compatibility.

Key Display: A 1.1-meter-wide HyperVision Panoramic Display serves as an advanced head-up display for both driver and passenger.

Flagship Autonomous Hardware, Standard

Every YU7 includes a full suite of advanced sensors, democratizing safety and future-proofing the vehicle. This multi-modal approach with LiDAR is a direct contrast to Tesla's vision-only system.

  • ● 128-line Hesai LiDAR
  • ● 4D Millimeter-Wave Radar
  • ● 11 High-Definition Cameras
  • ● 12 Ultrasonic Sensors
  • ● Nvidia DRIVE AGX Thor (700 TOPS)
