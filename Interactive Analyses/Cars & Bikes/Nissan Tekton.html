Intelligent Driving

Discover the seamless integration of technology designed to enhance every journey. Click on a feature to learn more.

ProPILOT 3.0 Assist

The most advanced driver-assistance system from Nissan. ProPILOT 3.0 offers hands-off single-lane driving on designated highways, as well as lane change assistance and predictive front collision warning. It uses a suite of high-resolution cameras and radar to create a real-time map of your surroundings, ensuring a safer, less stressful drive.

e-4ORCE All-Wheel Drive

Nissan's revolutionary dual-motor all-wheel-drive system. e-4ORCE delivers instant torque to all four wheels for unparalleled traction and control in all conditions. It independently manages power delivery and braking for each wheel, minimizing pitch and dive for a remarkably smooth and stable ride, whether you're accelerating, cornering, or braking.

Intelligent Power Grid

The Tekton is more than a vehicle; it's a mobile power source. With Vehicle-to-Grid (V2G) technology, your SUV can power your home during an outage or even sell energy back to the grid during peak hours. This bi-directional charging capability redefines the role of your vehicle in your home's energy ecosystem.
