The 2025 Ghost Series II

An Analysis of Post-Opulent Perfection.

The Philosophy of Ghost

The Ghost is guided by 'Post-Opulence'—a philosophy that rejects superfluous design for a distilled, monolithic, and unobtrusive form of luxury. It is luxury that whispers its quality rather than shouting its price, resonating with a new generation that values material integrity and engineering excellence over overt displays of wealth.

The 'Magic Carpet Ride'

More than a slogan, the 'Magic Carpet Ride' is an engineering promise. The Ghost doesn't just react to the road; it anticipates it. Through a suite of proactive technologies, it neutralizes imperfections before they reach the cabin, creating a sensation of serene flight that is unmatched.

⚏

Planar Suspension System

A world-first Upper Wishbone Damper and self-leveling air struts quell high-frequency vibrations for ultimate isolation.

🔍

Flagbearer System

Stereo cameras scan the road ahead, proactively adjusting the suspension for upcoming imperfections.

📞

Satellite Aided Transmission

GPS data is used to pre-select the optimal gear for upcoming corners, ensuring seamless power delivery.

The Uncompromising V12

At the heart of the Ghost lies the formidable 6.75-litre twin-turbocharged V12. The focus is not on raw numbers, but on the quality of its delivery—an effortless, near-silent wave of acceleration Rolls-Royce calls "waftability." Explore the performance characteristics of the Ghost and its more potent alter ego, the Black Badge.

The Black Badge Alter Ego

Not just a trim, Black Badge is a distinct identity. It is a daring expression of luxury and power for a more assertive clientele. With enhanced performance, a recalibrated chassis, and a subversive design language featuring darkened chrome and unique materials, it is the Ghost's rebellious soul made manifest.

  • ➜ Increased Output: Engine tuned to ~600 hp and 664 lb-ft of torque.
  • ➜ Sharpened Response: 'Low' mode increases gearshift speeds by 50%.
  • ➜ Authoritative Sound: Enhanced exhaust note for a more pronounced V12 sound.
  • ➜ Darkened Aesthetic: Black chrome Spirit of Ecstasy, Pantheon Grille, and unique forged wheels.
