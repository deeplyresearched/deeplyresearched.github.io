What's New for 2026?

Upgraded Digital Cockpit

A new, faster 11.2-inch "floating" touchscreen powered by a Snapdragon processor provides a fluid, responsive experience with Google Built-in for seamless navigation and voice control.

Refined Exterior Styling

The front grille is updated with a new diagonal motif from the flagship XC90, creating a stronger family resemblance. Darkened taillights and new wheel designs complete the modern look.

Standardized Safety Suite

In a class-leading move, Volvo's entire suite of advanced safety and driver-assistance systems, including Adaptive Cruise Control, is now standard on every XC60 trim level.

A Scandinavian Sanctuary

The 2026 XC60's cabin is a masterclass in human-centric design, blending cutting-edge technology with timeless materials and everyday practicality. This refresh introduces a faster, larger screen while retaining key physical controls, creating an ideal bridge for modern drivers.

Intuitive Technology

The new 11.2" display is 2x faster, offering a seamless interface with Google Built-in. Crucially, it's paired with a physical volume knob and defrost buttons for tactile, no-look operation of core functions.

Premium Materials

Experience new sustainable options like Quilted Nordico vegan upholstery or genuine Nappa leather. An Orrefors crystal gear shifter adds a unique touch of Swedish artistry to the center console.

Uncompromised Utility

Practicality remains a core strength, with a generous 63.3 cubic feet of maximum cargo space. The well-shaped cargo bay and standard power liftgate make loading effortless.

The Safety Mandate

While competitors charge thousands for optional packages, Volvo makes its entire suite of advanced safety systems standard on every 2026 XC60. Safety isn't a luxury; it's the foundation.

A Bridge to the Future

This refresh is more than an update; it's a strategic move. The XC60 serves as a profitable and popular bridge, funding the brand's all-electric future while providing a compelling option for today's luxury buyer.

American Backbone

Starting in late 2026, the XC60 will be produced at Volvo's advanced facility in South Carolina. This "build where you sell" strategy strengthens market roots, reduces delivery times, and insulates the brand from global supply chain volatility.

The Coexistence Strategy

The refreshed XC60 will sell alongside the upcoming all-electric EX60. This allows Volvo to serve customers who aren't ready for a full EV, using the profits from its bestseller to fund the next generation of electric vehicle technology.
