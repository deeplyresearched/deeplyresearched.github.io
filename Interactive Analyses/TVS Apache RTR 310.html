The Refinement Journey

TVS has proactively addressed initial feedback on engine vibrations through continuous software and hardware updates, showcasing a strong commitment to customer satisfaction.

Initial Launch

Engine criticized for significant vibrations and a "buzzy" character.

Software Updates

ECU re-calibration for a smoother, less jerky throttle response at low speeds.

Hardware Updates

Heavier bar-end dampers and denser foam padding added to absorb vibrations.

Current State

Vibrations significantly reduced to a "character trait" rather than a flaw.
