This has not been an isolated event; it has forced a global re-evaluation of supply chains, with Southeast Asia at the epicenter of both challenges and opportunities.

A Tale of Two Metrics: FDI vs. GDP

While many nations in the region have attracted significant Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) from relocating companies, the impact on overall Gross Domestic Product (GDP) growth has been more varied. This reflects the complex reality that new investment can also bring infrastructure strain and economic volatility.
